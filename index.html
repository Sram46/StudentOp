About StudentOp

StudentOp is a unified platform for college students to explore opportunities like internships, workshops, blogs, and Q&A — all in one place. It’s built to ensure that students don’t miss important opportunities during their academic journey.

Key Features

  • Personalized opportunity feed
  • Peer-written blogs and questions
  • Upload brochures, links, PDFs
  • Admin-reviewed content
  • Filters by department, semester, interest
  • Simple, distraction-free interface

Why StudentOp Exists

Students often miss out on great opportunities because the information is scattered across platforms like Telegram groups, college websites, and word of mouth. StudentOp solves this with one platform that connects juniors, seniors, and organizers in a trusted and useful way.
